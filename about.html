Our Story

A passion for perfection, a dedication to tradition

The Art of Baking & Brewing

Founded in 2018 by passionate bakers and coffee enthusiasts, Bread N' Br☕︎w represents the perfect union of artisan baking traditions and exceptional coffee culture in Berkeley Heights.

Local Heritage & Tradition

Our founders trained with master bakers and coffee roasters before bringing their expertise to Berkeley Heights. Every recipe and coffee blend has been carefully crafted to serve our local community with authenticity and warmth in every bite and sip.

Premium Sourcing

We source our ingredients from the finest local farms and trusted artisan producers. Our flour comes from regional mills, our coffee beans from sustainable farms worldwide, and our dairy from local New Jersey farms. This commitment to quality ingredients sets us apart.

Crafted with Care

Every croissant is laminated by hand, every tart shell rolled to perfection, and every coffee bean carefully roasted to bring out its unique characteristics. Our small-batch approach ensures that each item receives the individual attention it deserves.
